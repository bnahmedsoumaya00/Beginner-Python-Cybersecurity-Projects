What to Look For

URL Tricks

Pay attention to the URL bar. Real sites use exact domains like "google.com" while phishing sites use tricks like "gooogle.com" or "google-login.net"

Design Mimicry

Notice how perfectly these demos replicate real sites. Attackers invest heavily in making pages look authentic.

Autofill Capture

Watch how your browser automatically fills credentials. This happens instantly, often before you even notice.
