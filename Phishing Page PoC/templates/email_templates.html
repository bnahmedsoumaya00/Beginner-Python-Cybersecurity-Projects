How to Verify Legitimate Emails

  1. Check sender domain carefully - Look for typos or extra characters
  2. Hover over links - See real destination before clicking
  3. Go directly to the website - Type URL yourself instead of clicking
  4. Look for personalization - Real companies use your name
  5. Check for grammar errors - Professional companies proofread
  6. Verify unexpected requests - Call the company using official numbers
  7. Don't trust urgency - Legitimate companies give reasonable time
